# LaunchGrid — Per-Platform Growth & Reach Playbook (2026)

**Goal:** Maximum organic reach to **non-followers** from a brand-new, zero-follower account, with the honest offer **"First 10 stores set up free"** as the conversion hook. Budget = ₹0 now; small paid tests later (clearly labeled).
**Audience:** Indian SMB owners — kirana, boutiques, home bakers, resellers — Tier 1-3 cities, Hindi + English.
**Accounts:** X [@launchgrid_in](https://x.com/launchgrid_in) · Instagram [launchgrid.in](https://instagram.com/launchgrid.in) · Facebook page (in setup).

> Golden rule for a pre-revenue product: **sell the offer, never fake results.** No "we got X store ₹50k in 2 days." Instead: "Setting up the first 10 stores free — here's exactly what I build for you." Honesty *is* the differentiation against the fake-guru ecosystem your audience is sick of.

---

# 1) INSTAGRAM — your #1 channel for this audience

Instagram + Reels is where Indian SMB owners discover tools. This gets the most effort.

### 1. Algorithm & reach levers (2026)
- **Reels are the only real non-follower engine.** Reels up to **3 minutes** now get recommended to non-followers via Explore + the Reels feed. Carousels/static rarely reach beyond followers. ([Buffer](https://buffer.com/resources/instagram-algorithms/), [TrueFuture Media](https://www.truefuturemedia.com/articles/instagram-reels-reach-2026-business-growth-guide))
- **Watch time is the #1 ranking signal** (confirmed by Adam Mosseri). The order that matters: **watch time → sends-per-reach (DM shares) → likes-per-reach.** A 15-sec Reel at 80% completion beats a 3-min Reel at 20%. ([dataslayer](https://www.dataslayer.ai/blog/instagram-algorithm-2025-complete-guide-for-marketers), [Later](https://later.com/blog/how-instagram-algorithm-works/))
- **Hook the first 3 seconds.** If most viewers pass the 3-sec mark, Instagram widens distribution. ([Buffer](https://buffer.com/resources/instagram-algorithms/))
- **Sends/shares (DMs) are the strongest "push me wider" lever** — engineer "send this to a friend who runs a shop" moments.
- **Trending audio gives a distribution boost.** Add a rising sound to every Reel (even talking-head ones — keep it low in the mix). ([Sprout Social](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/instagram-algorithm/))

### 2. Account settings that maximize reach (exact toggles)
- Switch to a **Business** account: *Settings → Account type and tools → Switch to professional account → Business → category "Software"/"Product/Service."*
- **Turn ON recommendations:** *Settings → Suggested content / "Recommend your content on other profiles"* — leave **enabled** (it's the toggle that lets Explore surface you to non-followers).
- **Don't add the website link in a way that suppresses Reels** — keep links in **bio** (link-in-bio tool), not pasted in captions.
- Fill the **bio with a keyword line** Instagram now indexes for search: e.g. `Online dukaan in 10 min · Free store setup (first 10) · Hindi support`.
- Connect to the Facebook Page via **Meta Business Suite** (see FB section) so you can cross-post and later run ads from one place.
- Set a branded **profile name field** to "LaunchGrid — Online Store Builder" (the name field is searchable; the @handle is not as much).

### 3. Hashtag strategy
- **2026 rule: 3–5 hashtags MAX.** Instagram enforces a 5-tag cap and treats >5 as a low-intent spam signal. Hashtags now **categorize**, they don't boost — so be precise, not broad. ([Ingeniom](https://www.ingeniom.com/post/instagram-recommends-three-to-five-hashtags-2026), [EvergreenFeed](https://www.evergreenfeed.com/blog/how-many-hashtags-for-instagram/))
- Mix: **1 broad + 2-3 niche.** Niche (10K–500K posts) is the sweet spot.

**Ready-to-use sets (pick one per post, rotate):**
- **Set A (general SMB):** `#SmallBusinessIndia #DukaanOnline #VocalForLocal #OnlineStoreIndia #ChhotaBusiness`
- **Set B (home bakers / boutiques):** `#HomeBakerIndia #BoutiqueIndia #InstaShopIndia #MadeInIndia #SmallBusinessOwner`
- **Set C (resellers / kirana):** `#ResellerIndia #KiranaStore #OnlineDukaan #StartupIndia #SmallBizIndia`

### 4. Posting cadence & timing (IST)
- **Cadence:** 1 Reel/day is ideal; floor of **4–5 Reels/week**. Add 2-3 Stories/day (Stories keep existing followers warm; Reels bring new ones).
- **Best windows:** weekday **7–9 PM IST** (peak, ~70% of users active) and **10 AM–1 PM**. Wednesday 8 PM is the strongest single slot. Post **Reels slightly earlier, 6–8 PM**. Weekend sweet spot **11 AM–1 PM**. ([StartUpMandi](https://startupmandi.in/news/tips-tricks/best-time-to-post-on-instagram-in-india/), [Brand Update](https://brand-update.com/resources/best-time-to-post-instagram-india))

### 5. Content formats that win
- **Reels (90% of effort):** screen-recording of building a store in real time; "type your product, get a store" demos; before/after (WhatsApp-catalog chaos → clean store).
- **Carousels (10%):** "5 mistakes home bakers make selling on Instagram" educational saves — drive *saves* (a quality signal).
- **Static:** almost never; only for offer announcements.

### 6. Hooks (5) — honest, offer-led
1. **(Hyper-specificity)** "Main aapki online dukaan 11 minute mein bana doonga — bina coding, bina ₹1 kharch." *(I'll build your online store in 11 minutes — no code, ₹0.)*
2. **(Contradiction)** "Aapko website ke liye developer ki zaroorat NAHI hai. Yeh dekho." *(You do NOT need a developer for a website. Watch.)*
3. **(Timeframe tension)** "Pehle 10 dukaandaar — main khud aapki store free mein set up karunga. Counter chalu hai." *(First 10 shopkeepers — I'll set up your store free, myself. Counter's running.)*
4. **(POV-as-advice)** "POV: aap WhatsApp pe orders sambhalte-sambhalte thak gaye ho. Ek link se sab automatic ho jaata hai."
5. **(Hyper-specificity)** "Home baker ho? Yeh 3 cheezein aapki online store mein honi chahiye — main free bana ke deta hoon."

### 7. Paid (WHEN YOU HAVE BUDGET)
- **Highest-ROI play:** boost your single best-performing organic Reel via **Meta Ads Manager** (not the in-app Boost button) → objective **Engagement/Traffic**, audience = **interest targeting: "Small business owners," "Entrepreneurship," "Etsy/Shopify," "Online shopping"** narrowed to India, age 22-45.
- **Budget:** ₹200–300/day for 4–5 days (₹1,000–1,500 test). Only ever boost a Reel that *already* beat your average organically.
- **Watch:** cost-per-link-click and profile visits — not likes.

### 8. What to measure (predicts customers)
- **Profile visits** and **link/bio clicks** (intent).
- **DM "STORE" / reply count** — add a CTA "comment STORE and I'll DM the free setup link."
- **Saves + sends per Reel** (predicts reach *and* serious buyers).
- **Ignore:** raw like count, follower count.

# 2) X (TWITTER) — build-in-public, founder voice

Honest note up front: **X is a weaker discovery channel for kirana/boutique owners** (most aren't on X). Its value here is **build-in-public credibility, founder network, and Indian startup/SMB-Twitter** — useful for partnerships, press, and early adopters, not bulk merchant acquisition.

### 1. Algorithm & reach levers (2026)
- **Replies are king.** Author replying to their own thread = **+75**, the single most powerful signal. A reply is worth ~27× a like; a full conversation ~150× a like. ([posteverywhere](https://posteverywhere.ai/blog/how-the-x-twitter-algorithm-works), [OpenTweet](https://opentweet.io/blog/how-twitter-x-algorithm-works-2026))
- **First 30–60 min velocity decides distribution.** 20 replies in 30 min beats 50 over a day. ([posteverywhere](https://posteverywhere.ai/blog/how-the-x-twitter-algorithm-works))
- **External links are penalized 30–50%; for non-Premium accounts, near-zero reach since March 2026.** **Put the link in the FIRST REPLY, not the post.** ([teract](https://www.teract.ai/resources/twitter-algorithm-2026), [posteverywhere](https://posteverywhere.ai/blog/how-the-x-twitter-algorithm-works))

### 2. Account settings that maximize reach
- **X Premium (Basic ₹/mo)** materially boosts reach + lets you write longer posts/threads and edit. **This is the one paid lever worth it on X** because non-Premium link posts are now nearly invisible. (Label: low-budget paid.)
- Set a clear **bio + pinned post**: pinned = the "first 10 free" offer thread with link in the first reply.
- Add **website + location (India)** in profile.
- Turn on **Creator/Professional account** for analytics.

### 3. Hashtag strategy
- **X hates hashtag spam. Use 0–1, max 2.** Often best to use **none** and rely on keywords (X search is semantic now). If using one: `#BuildInPublic` (founder reach) or `#SmallBusinessIndia`.
- **3 minimal sets:** (A) *none — pure text* (B) `#BuildInPublic` (C) `#VocalForLocal` — and that's it.

### 4. Posting cadence & timing (IST)
- **Cadence:** 1–3 posts/day + reply to 10–15 others daily (replies are your real growth engine here).
- **Timing:** Tue/Wed/Thu **8–11 AM IST** and **11 AM–4 PM**; Wednesday is the single best day. Weekend reach is poor for B2B — skip Sunday. ([Sprout Social](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/best-times-to-post-on-twitter/), [Buffer](https://buffer.com/resources/best-time-to-post-on-twitter-x/))

### 5. Content formats that win
- **Text-first posts and threads** outperform link/image posts. Build-in-public: "Day 7 of building a no-code store tool for Indian shops — here's what broke today."
- **Native screen-record clips** (short) do well; avoid posting bare external links.
- **Threads** for "how I'd set up an online store for a home baker, step by step."

### 6. Hooks (5)
1. **(Timeframe tension)** "I'm setting up the first 10 Indian small-business stores for free this month. 6 slots left. 🧵"
2. **(Contradiction)** "Your business does NOT need a ₹30,000 website. I'll prove it by building one live for a kirana store. 👇"
3. **(Hyper-specificity)** "Building a no-code online-store builder for Indian dukaandaars. Today: store live in 11 min, ₹0 spent. Watch:"
4. **(POV-as-advice)** "If you sell on WhatsApp and copy-paste your catalog 50 times a day — this thread is for you."
5. **(Build-in-public honesty)** "Pre-launch, zero customers, zero revenue. So I'm doing the first 10 store setups myself, free, in public. Follow the journey."

### 7. Paid (WHEN YOU HAVE BUDGET)
- **Honest verdict: X Ads are weak/expensive for this audience — do NOT spend ad budget here.** The only paid play worth it is **X Premium** for the reach un-throttle + link survivability. Put paid rupees into Meta instead.

### 8. What to measure
- **Profile clicks** and **first-reply link clicks** (intent).
- **Reply count in first hour** (predicts whether it spreads).
- **DMs from founders/partners** (X's real value = relationships).
- **Ignore:** impressions, follower count.

# 3) FACEBOOK — Groups are the hidden goldmine

For Tier 2-3 India, **Facebook Groups** are where SMB owners actually hang out (reseller groups, "Women Entrepreneurs India," city business groups). The Page matters less than the Groups.

### 1. Algorithm & reach levers (2026)
- **Group content out-distributes Page content.** ~1.8B monthly Group users; Group posts rank higher in Feed than Page posts. **Your reach engine is participating in Groups, not posting to your Page.** ([Omnichat](https://blog.omnichat.ai/facebook-algorithm-increase-reach/), [fbgroupbulkposter](https://fbgroupbulkposter.com/blog/facebook-organic-reach-2026))
- **Private shares > likes.** One Share-to-Story or Save is worth more than ~50 likes. Engineer share-worthy, reference content. ([Omnichat](https://blog.omnichat.ai/facebook-algorithm-increase-reach/))
- **Native video & Reels get 2–3× the reach of photos** and dominate organic distribution. **Never post YouTube links** — upload native. ([posteverywhere](https://posteverywhere.ai/blog/how-the-facebook-algorithm-works))
- **Reply to every comment in the first 60 min** to trigger the "conversation thread" boost. ([cloudixdigital](https://cloudixdigital.com/a-guide-to-organic-reach-how-the-facebook-algorithm-works-in-2026/))
- **Saves** are now a strong quality signal → post checklists/guides people bookmark.

### 2. Account settings that maximize reach
- Finish the **Page setup:** category "Software Company" / "Product/Service," full About with keywords, CTA button = **"Send Message"** (WhatsApp-style concierge) or "Sign Up."
- **Link Instagram ↔ Facebook in Meta Business Suite:** *Business Suite → Settings → Accounts → connect both* — enables cross-posting Reels and one-place ads.
- **Enable Facebook Reels** cross-posting from Instagram (Business Suite toggle) so every IG Reel auto-appears on FB.
- Add the **WhatsApp number** to the Page so the "Send Message" button opens WhatsApp.
- **Join 8–12 relevant Indian Groups** (reseller, women entrepreneurs, city startup, home-baker, kirana). This is the setup that actually drives reach.

### 3. Hashtag strategy
- **Facebook barely uses hashtags. Use 0–2, only for tracking/branding.** They don't drive discovery.
- **3 minimal sets:** (A) *none* (B) `#VocalForLocal #SmallBusinessIndia` (C) `#DukaanOnline` — keep it to 1-2 if any.

### 4. Posting cadence & timing (IST)
- **Page:** 3–5 native posts/week (don't overpost a 0-follower Page).
- **Groups:** value-first comments/posts **daily** in 2-3 groups (rotate so you're not spammy).
- **Timing:** weekday **7–9 PM IST** and **1–3 PM**; align with IG windows for cross-posts.

### 5. Content formats that win
- **Native video / Reels** (highest reach) — same clips as IG.
- **Text + image "story" posts** ("Met a saree reseller in Jaipur drowning in WhatsApp orders — here's the store we built her, free") — authentic > polished.
- **Checklist/guide posts** for saves and Group value.
- **Avoid:** link-only posts (suppressed); put the link in the first comment.

### 6. Hooks (5)
1. **(POV-as-advice)** "Running your shop on WhatsApp screenshots? Here's the free fix I'm offering the first 10 businesses."
2. **(Hyper-specificity)** "I built a saree reseller in Jaipur a full online store in 11 minutes. ₹0. Here's exactly how (and I'll do yours free)."
3. **(Contradiction)** "You don't need Amazon or a ₹25,000 website to sell online. Read this before you pay anyone."
4. **(Timeframe tension)** "First 10 store setups this month are on me — free. 6 spots left. Comment 'STORE'."
5. **(POV-as-advice)** "Home baker, boutique owner, reseller — if customers say 'send me the link' and you don't have one, this post is for you."

### 7. Paid (WHEN YOU HAVE BUDGET)
- **Highest-ROI play on this whole list:** Meta-boosted **native Reel/video** via Ads Manager, objective **Leads or Messages (WhatsApp)**, audience = **interest "Small business owners / Entrepreneurship / Online selling"** narrowed to India, Tier 2-3 cities, age 24-45. Facebook + Instagram placements together (one campaign covers both).
- **Budget:** ₹200–500/day, 5–7 day test (₹1,500–3,500). Start ₹200/day.
- **Watch:** cost-per-WhatsApp-message-started / cost-per-lead. Under ~₹40-60 per qualified message = working.

### 8. What to measure
- **Messages started (WhatsApp) / "STORE" comments** — direct intent.
- **Shares + saves** (predict organic reach next time).
- **Link clicks to launchgrid.in.**
- **Ignore:** Page likes, post reactions.
