# LaunchGrid hook library — built on 4 viral patterns

Principle: the best hooks don't feel like hooks. Every line below reads like a human moment,
not an ad. Use one hook per post — first line on IG (only ~125 chars show before "more"),
first sentence on X, spoken/overlaid in first 1.5s of a Reel.

## Pattern 1 — Contradiction & contrast (unresolved tension)

1. "Mera product sasta nahi hai. Phir bhi pehle 10 dukaano ka setup main FREE kar raha hoon."
2. "I built an online store for a man who hates technology. He got his first order before he finished learning to open the app."
3. "She said 'main online-valine nahi karti.' Her store did ₹4,000 the first weekend."
4. "Scared to put your shop online? Good. The fear means you've built something worth selling."
5. "It took him 40 years to build the shop. And 15 minutes to put it online."
6. "I don't want you to buy anything. I want to build your store for free. (Yes, there's a catch: you have to send me 5 photos.)"
7. "The least techy person I know now checks his orders before his WhatsApp."

## Pattern 2 — Hyper-specificity (speak to ONE person)

8. "If you've ever typed 'price inbox kar diya hai' for the 47th time today — this is for you."
9. "For the boutique owner who sends 12 photos one by one to every customer who asks 'aur designs?'"
10. "You know that notebook? Blue pen for COD orders, red pen for paid. That notebook is losing you money."
11. "If your 'website' is a WhatsApp DP with your phone number written on a poster..."
12. "To the home baker who lost an order because she saw the message 6 hours late."
13. "That one customer who says 'bhaiya GST bill milega?' — this post is about him."
14. "If you've ever screenshot your own WhatsApp status to forward it to one more group..."
15. "For everyone whose 'order book' is 14 different WhatsApp chats scrolled at midnight."

## Pattern 3 — Timeframe tension

16. "A 40-year-old saree shop went online in 14 minutes."
17. "Setup: 15 minutes. First order: 11 days. The excuse: 3 years."
18. "Your chai break is longer than the time it takes to put your dukaan online."
19. "Day 1: 5 product photos on WhatsApp. Day 9: first online order from a customer 600 km away."
20. "In the time it takes to watch 3 reels, your shop could be taking orders."
21. "100 stores in 100 days. This is day 1." (series anchor)

## Pattern 4 — POV (advice disguised as scenario)

22. "POV: it's 11pm, the shutter is down, and an order just came in anyway."
23. "POV: customer asks 'price kya hai' and you send ONE link instead of 12 photos."
24. "POV: you stopped being your own delivery-status call center."
25. "POV: the GST bill makes itself while you pack the order."
26. "POV: your regular customer's sister in Pune just ordered — and you didn't lift a finger."
27. "POV: you finally know which product people look at most (it's not the one you thought)."

## Usage map

| Surface | Pattern that performs | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| X posts | 1 (contradiction) + 3 (timeframe) | daily build-in-public |
| IG feed captions | 2 (specificity) first line | 3×/week |
| Reels / Status (with trending audio, in-app only) | 4 (POV) as text overlay | daily |
| Proof posts after each real store | 3 + real numbers | every setup |

Rules: never sound like an ad in line 1; the brand name appears AFTER the hook lands;
one CTA only — DM "STORE"; numbers must be real once we have real merchants.
