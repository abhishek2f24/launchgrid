# Facebook Groups kit — the fastest organic path to your first merchant

Per REACH-PLAYBOOK.md, Facebook *Groups* (not your Page) are where Indian Tier-2/3
shop owners, resellers and home businesses actually gather. This is the highest-leverage
*organic* channel for finding customer #1 — but it only works if you participate as a
helpful member, not a spammer. Groups ban link-droppers instantly.

## The rule that keeps you un-banned
Give value 4 times before you ask once. Most groups forbid promo except on specific
days or in specific posts. Read each group's rules first. Your goal in a group is to
become "the person who helps people get their shop online," not "the person selling X."

## Step 1 — Join these groups (search these terms on Facebook)
- "Reseller India" / "Meesho Resellers" / "Reselling Business India"
- "[Your city] Small Business" / "[Your city] Entrepreneurs"
- "Home Bakers India" / "Home Business India" / "Women Entrepreneurs India"
- "Boutique Owners India" / "Saree Business" / "Online Business India"
- "Startup India Founders" / "Digital India Small Business"
Join 6–10. Set notifications to "Highlights" on the 3 most active.

## Step 2 — Warm up for 2–3 days (no promotion)
- Answer 5–10 questions where you genuinely know the answer: GST thresholds, COD vs prepaid,
  how to take UPI payments, how to share a catalog on WhatsApp, why marketplaces eat margin.
- Comment helpfully. React. Become a familiar name. This builds the trust that converts later.

## Step 4 — When someone replies "STORE"
Move to DM/WhatsApp. Run the conversion checklist in FIRST-CUSTOMER-KIT.md:
get shop name + 5 photos + prices + UPI + delivery area → build with them in 15 minutes →
make them share the link on their status. That first live store is the sale.

## Honesty guardrails (you're pre-revenue)
- Never claim other merchants' results ("Ramesh got 11 orders") — you have none yet.
- Sell the OFFER (free setup, first 10) and the PRODUCT (what it does), not fake outcomes.
- The moment you have a real merchant with real orders (with their permission), THAT
  becomes your proof post — and it'll convert 10x harder than any promise.
