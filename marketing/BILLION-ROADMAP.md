## PHASE 5 — 2030+: the billion-dollar math (honest version)

A $1B Indian B2B company needs roughly one of:
- **$80–100M ARR at ~10x** (SaaS multiple) → ~550K paying merchants at ₹1,200/mo blended, OR
- **Payments/credit at scale**: ~$15–20B annualized GMV at a 0.5–0.7% blended take (GoKwik: $450M valuation at $30M ARR shows the multiple works), OR
- Both layers compounding (the Razorpay path: payments → banking → lending → $7.5B).

India has ~60M micro-merchants; 550K paying = 0.9% penetration. The market allows it. The constraint
is execution speed and surviving long enough — which loops back to Phase 0: **everything above is
gated by the first 100 paying merchants. The roadmap to a billion starts with 20 WhatsApp messages.**
