# First Customer Kit — get one business onboarded this week

Target: ONE business owner signs up and gets their store set up. Everything below is
zero-budget. Your unfair advantage: **the concierge offer** — you personally set up
their store in 15 minutes while they watch. Agencies charge ₹15–50k for this; you do
it free and they pay only when they grow (plan upgrade).

---

## The offer (say this everywhere)

> **"Main aapki dukaan 15 minute me online kar dunga — bilkul free.**
> Aap sirf 5 product photos bhejiye WhatsApp pe. UPI payment, Cash on Delivery,
> GST bill — sab built-in. Pasand na aaye toh delete kar dijiye. Koi charge nahi."

English version:
> "I'll put your shop online in 15 minutes — completely free. Just send me 5 product
> photos on WhatsApp. UPI payments, COD, GST invoices all built in. Don't like it?
> Delete it. No charge."

Why it works: zero risk, zero effort for them, concrete time promise, and you're a
person — not a SaaS landing page.

---

## Channel 1: WhatsApp direct outreach (fastest — do TODAY)

You already know business owners: relatives, friends' shops, your local market,
college contacts who resell. List 20 of them. Send this, personalised:

**Script A — someone you know:**
> Bhaiya/Didi, ek cheez banayi hai maine — LaunchGrid. Aapki shop ka online store
> 15 min me ban jata hai, WhatsApp pe order aate hain, UPI/COD sab chalta hai.
> Main khud aapka store bana ke deta hoon, free me. 5 product photos bhejo bas.
> Demo: launchgrid.in

**Script B — shop you visit (in person, then WhatsApp):**
> Visit the shop, buy something small, then: "Aapka Instagram/WhatsApp pe customer
> aate hain? Main online store bana deta hoon free me — order aur payment automatic
> ho jayega. 15 minute lagenge, abhi bana ke dikhata hoon."

**Script C — Instagram reseller (DM):**
> Hi! Saw your page — products are great. DM-to-order must take so much time.
> I can set up a proper store for you (free) — customers browse, pay UPI or choose
> COD, you get a notification. Same link goes in your bio. Want me to set it up?
> Takes 15 min. Example: launchgrid.in/sell-online/sarees

**Follow-up (48h later, if no reply):**
> Maine ek sample store bana diya aapke liye — [link]. 2 minute dekh lijiye,
> pasand aaye toh main products daal deta hoon.
> (Actually create a store with their shop name + 2-3 products from their Insta photos.
> Seeing THEIR OWN shop online converts better than any pitch.)

## Conversion checklist (when someone says yes)

1. Get: shop name, 5 product photos + prices, their UPI/phone, delivery area.
2. You create the account WITH them on a call (their email/phone — it's their store).
3. Add products, set COD on, share the store link back: `theirname.launchgrid.in`.
4. Make them share it on their WhatsApp status immediately ("first 5 customers get X").
5. Their first order = your case study. Screenshot (with permission) → Post 2 above.

## What counts as the FIRST SALE
A real business owner with their own account, their own products live, actively
sharing their store link. (Plan upgrade comes later — don't push it on day one;
the catalog-cap and GST-invoice nudges in the product do that job.)
