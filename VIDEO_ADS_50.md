# LaunchGrid — 50 Video Ad Concepts (scripts · hooks · scene breakdowns)
**Format note:** Each entry = Hook (first 2 seconds — spoken or text-on-screen) → Beats (scenes) → CTA. All 9:16 vertical unless noted. House rules: no fake earnings claims, no fake countdowns; the villain is hesitation; UPI ping = the brand sound.
**AI-generation note:** ★ marked concepts are single-scene, atmosphere-driven — ideal for AI text-to-video (no lip-sync, no UI text dependence). Dialogue/screen-recording concepts need real footage or heavy editing.

---

## A. The Emotional Hook (dream → action)

**1. ★ The Ping at 11:43 PM** — *Hook:* dark bedroom, phone lights up.
Beats: (1) Sleeping city apartment, phone on bedside table glows. (2) Close-up: payment notification, soft "ping". (3) A hand reaches, face lit by screen — a slow smile. CTA text: *Know the second someone buys. LaunchGrid.*

**2. ★ Two Years of Someday** — *Hook:* a sticky note that says "business idea" yellowing on a wall.
Beats: (1) Time-lapse: seasons pass over a desk, note fades. (2) Hand finally pulls it off. (3) Cut to phone: "Your store is live." CTA: *Done thinking? 15 minutes.*

**3. ★ The Shutter** — *Hook:* an old shop shutter rolls down at dusk.
Beats: (1) Street shop closes, owner walks home. (2) On the bus, opens phone — orders arriving. (3) The "shop" is now a glowing storefront on screen. CTA: *Your shutter never closes.*

**4. The Montage of Excuses** — *Hook (VO):* "I'll start after Diwali. After exams. After the wedding…"
Beats: calendar pages flip with each excuse; freeze on today's date; phone slides in: "Type your idea." CTA: *Your 'after' is now.*

**5. ★ Mother's Recipes** — *Hook:* hands kneading dough, flour in the air, golden light.
Beats: (1) Kitchen close-ups, pickle jars, masala tins. (2) A daughter photographs a jar on her phone. (3) Order notification pings; mother's surprised laugh (no dialogue). CTA: *Her recipes deserve a checkout button.*

**6. The First Customer Is Mom** — *Hook:* "My first order was from my mother. My second wasn't."
Beats: WhatsApp share to family group → first order (mom) → stranger's order arrives → genuine shock. CTA: *Your first 10 customers are in your contacts.*

**7. ★ Hostel Room HQ** — *Hook:* cramped hostel room, laptop balanced on a bucket.
Beats: (1) Student between textbooks checks phone. (2) Order ping during lecture notes. (3) Packs a parcel under fairy lights. CTA: *College + business. Both possible.*

**8. The 2-Minute Silence** — *Hook:* total silence, black screen, text: "This is how long your excuse lasts."
Beats: silence → UPI ping breaks it → dashboard montage at speed. CTA: *Start louder.*

**9. ★ Rain Delivery** — *Hook:* monsoon street, a delivery bag glows under a plastic cover.
Beats: courier in rain → merchant watches "out for delivery" from a chai stall → delivered ✓. CTA: *Your business runs even when you're standing still.*

**10. One Year From Now** — *Hook:* split screen: "You, today" / "You, one year from now."
Beats: left side scrolls reels; right side packs orders, checks dashboard, smiles. Sides swap when left taps "Build my store". CTA: *Same year. Different you.*

## B. Product Proof (show, don't claim)

**11. The 15-Minute Speedrun (real-time)** — *Hook:* "No cuts. Timer's on."
Beats: full screen-record: idea typed → store generated → product imported → live link shared, clock overlay 00:00→14:32. CTA: *Beat my time.*

**12. ★ The Materialize** — *Hook:* an empty white room.
Beats: (1) Products fade in onto invisible shelves one by one. (2) A storefront frame assembles around them. (3) A door appears; an "OPEN" sign flips. CTA: *Type your idea. Watch your store appear.*

**13. Paste Link → Product** — *Hook:* "Watch me steal my own product from Meesho." 
Beats: copy Meesho URL → paste in LaunchGrid → photos/title/price appear → set margin slider → live. CTA: *Stop uploading. Start importing.*

**14. The Camera Trick** — *Hook:* "This photo became a product page in 40 seconds."
Beats: phone photographs earrings on a dupatta → app fills the page → share sheet → first view arrives. CTA: *Photo to product. 60 seconds.*

**15. COD Without Fear** — *Hook:* "COD orders used to scare me."
Beats: fake-order horror flashback (grey) → OTP shield animation blocks it (color) → verified order ships. CTA: *Sell the way India buys — safely.*

**16. ★ The Funnel Comes Alive** — *Hook:* a dark dashboard, one dot of light.
Beats: visitor dots flow down a glowing funnel → cart → checkout → ping! order lands, confetti of light. Lo-fi beat. CTA: *Watch your business breathe.*

**17. Abandoned Cart Rescue** — *Hook:* "She left ₹1,299 in her cart at 4 PM."
Beats: cart abandoned → WhatsApp recovery message auto-sends → 4:32 PM: payment ping. CTA: *LaunchGrid doesn't let money walk away.*

**18. GST Without the Fear** — *Hook:* a scary government envelope on a desk.
Beats: envelope looms (drums) → cut to calm dashboard: "₹12.4L of ₹40L threshold" → exhale. CTA: *We watch the thresholds. You sell.*

**19. The Bio Link Upgrade** — *Hook:* an Instagram bio that says "DM to order 🙏".
Beats: 47 "price?" DMs flood (chaos) → bio changes to store link → orders arrive silently. CTA: *One link. Zero 'price?' DMs.*

**20. Dashboard Day in 15 Seconds** — *Hook:* sunrise time-lapse over a phone.
Beats: dawn: 0 visitors → noon: 38 → evening: 2 orders → night: revenue card glows. CTA: *Your whole business. One glance.*

## C. The Comparison Lane (honest, never bitter)

**21. The Dollar Bill Problem** — *Hook:* "Why is my Indian store paying in dollars?"
Beats: Shopify invoice in USD + FX fee highlighted → LaunchGrid invoice in ₹ → side-by-side math. CTA: *INR in. INR out.*

**22. Commission Calculator** — *Hook:* "Sell ₹1 lakh on a marketplace. Keep how much?"
Beats: animated calculator: marketplace commissions slice the stack of notes → LaunchGrid: 0% slice. CTA: *Own your margin.*

**23. The Plugin Mountain** — *Hook:* "To accept UPI on Shopify you need…"
Beats: app-install screens stack into a teetering tower → tower collapses → LaunchGrid: "UPI. Built in." CTA: *No plugins. Just selling.*

**24. Marketplace vs Your Place** — *Hook:* a stall in a crowded bazaar vs a shop with your name on it.
Beats: bazaar stall (your products, someone else's roof, rent rising) → own storefront, customer list in hand. CTA: *Stop renting shelf space.*

**25. The Switch (testimonial format)** — *Hook:* "I moved my store on a Sunday afternoon."
Beats: real merchant talks 15s: why they left [platform], the import, first week. (Needs real consented merchant.) CTA: *Switching is the easy part.*

## D. Mobile App / Order-Alert Lane

**26. ★ Lock Screen Hero** — *Hook:* a phone face-down on a dinner table, family eating.
Beats: phone buzzes face-down → flipped: "New order — ₹1,299" → quiet fist-pump under the table. CTA: *Know the second someone buys. Get the app.*

**27. One-Tap Fulfil** — *Hook:* "Order to shipped in 11 seconds."
Beats: notification → tap → "Mark as packed" → WhatsApp tracking sent → done, phone pocketed. CTA: *Run it from your pocket.*

**28. ★ The Commute** — *Hook:* crowded local train, hands gripping the rail.
Beats: one hand holds rail, other checks orders → marks fulfilled between stations → doors open, smile. CTA: *Your office is wherever you are.*

**29. The Volume Off Test** — *Hook:* "My friends ask why my phone is never silent."
Beats: phone pings in cinema pocket / gym / wedding → each time a subtle smile. CTA: *That sound > your alarm.* (15s, comedic timing)

**30. ★ Night Market Glow** — *Hook:* a street food night market, neon and steam.
Beats: vendor flips momos → phone propped against sauce bottle shows incoming online order → packs one extra box. CTA: *Two counters. One business.*

## F. Pattern Interrupts & Formats

**41. The Anti-Guru** — *Hook:* "I won't promise you ₹1 lakh a month."
Beats: direct-to-camera honesty: "I can't promise sales. I can promise you'll be ready for them in 15 minutes." Show the readiness score climbing. CTA: *No course. No promises. A store.*

**42. ★ Object Metaphor: Seed** — *Hook:* macro shot, a seed dropped into dark soil.
Beats: time-lapse sprout → sapling → small plant with a price tag blooming like a flower. CTA: *Plant the idea today.*

**43. Comment-to-Store (UGC engine)** — *Hook:* "Top comment gets a free store built live."
Beats: read a real comment ("sell gym tees?") → build it live in 60s → tag the commenter. CTA: *Comment your idea.* (series format)

**44. The Reverse Unboxing** — *Hook:* hands seal a parcel instead of opening one.
Beats: tape gun, bubble wrap, address label, proud pat on the box — shot like an ASMR unboxing in reverse. CTA: *The other side of unboxing feels better.*

**45. ★ 100 Stores Time-lapse** — *Hook:* a map of India, dark.
Beats: dots of light appear city by city as stores launch, each with a tiny ping → constellation forms. CTA: *India is launching. Join it.* (use real store counts only)

**46. POV: First Sale** — *Hook:* POV shot — your hands holding chai, phone on table.
Beats: ping → you nearly drop the chai → fumble to open → "Order #001" → call mom. (Actor, 15s.) CTA: *Order #001 hits different.*

**47. ★ The Empty Chair Interview** — *Hook:* an empty chair under a single light, text: "We interviewed the business you didn't start."
Beats: chair stays empty through interview questions on screen → final question: "Why not?" → chair replaced by a glowing phone. CTA: *It's waiting.*

**48. Speedrun Challenge (creator collab)** — *Hook:* "Store launch speedrun. World record attempt."
Beats: creator races the 15-min claim, gaming-stream overlay, chat reactions. CTA: *Beat [creator]'s time.*

**49. The Refund** — *Hook:* "Day 6 of my free trial. Here's what happened."
Beats: honest diary: visitors graph, first cart, the nervous wait, the decision to pay. No hype. CTA: *7 days. Decide with data.*

**50. ★ Diwali Lamp** — *Hook:* a single diya being lit in the dark (festival flight).
Beats: one diya lights another (each = a store going live) → courtyard full of flames → one phone screen joins the lights with an order ping. CTA: *This festive season, light yours.*
