0 – 1.5s Scene 1 · Hook — The Price Slap ▼
Spoken script (voice-over)
"Website banane ke liye ₹50,000 kharcha kiya? Galti ho gayi."
Visual — what's on screen
Big white text on dark BG: ₹50,000 with a red strikethrough appearing in real-time. Camera is slightly handheld/shaky for authenticity.
On-screen caption text
"WEBSITE ₹50,000?! 😱" — Bold, center frame, safe zone.
Camera / edit style
Jump cut from black to text card in 0.3s. No logo. No music intro. Hit the word immediately.
Audio
SFX: cash register sound or error buzzer at "₹50,000". Voice-over starts instantly, no silence.
Pattern interruptShock triggerMoney triggerNo greeting
1.5 – 3s Scene 2 · Hook Payoff — Identify the Problem ▼
Spoken script
"Agency ne sirf ek website di — aur kuch nahi. Orders? Zero. Leads? Zero."
Visual
Cut to: a person (Indian small shop owner, could be you talking to camera) looking frustrated. OR a WhatsApp chat showing "0 orders" message thread — screen recording style.
On-screen text
"No orders. No leads. Just a website." — appearing word by word.
Visual change at 2s
Zoom into the WhatsApp screen at the 2s mark for retention. Caption flips to new text simultaneously.
Emotion target
Relatability — every local business owner has felt this exact pain.
Problem clear in <3sRelatabilityFOMO trigger
3 – 7s Scene 3 · Agitate — Make the Pain Worse ▼
Spoken script
"Aur agency boli — Google Ads ke liye alag paisa do. SEO ke liye alag. WhatsApp ke liye alag setup. Sab kuch alag alag."
Visual
Rapid-fire text cards flashing one by one: — "Google Ads: ₹10,000/mo" — "SEO: ₹8,000/mo" — "WhatsApp Setup: ₹5,000" Each one slams into frame with a SFX hit.
Edit technique
Each price card: hard cut every 1.2s. Add shake/vibrate effect on each card to amplify shock. Running total counter climbing in corner.
On-screen text
"₹73,000+ just to START?" — shown as the running total hits the number. Red color. Bold.
Emotion
Outrage + Shock. Business owner should feel validated in their frustration.
Agitate phaseVisual change every 1.2sShock + Money
7 – 10s Scene 4 · Bridge — The Contrast Setup ▼
Spoken script
"Toh fir kya karna chahiye? Ek platform mein sab kuch — website, store, payments, WhatsApp, SEO — woh bhi Indian price mein."
Visual
Transition from red/dark agitation cards to a clean, bright LaunchGrid dashboard — green tones. The mood lifts visually. Like sunlight after darkness.
On-screen text
"Ek platform. Sab kuch." (center frame, clean white text on brand color BG)
Audio shift
Music rises slightly here — uplifting. The tone flip is intentional. Viewer should feel relief.
SEO note
Keyword "website" spoken again here. Second SEO hit within 10s.
Bridge / pivot momentAspiration triggerTransformation setup
10 – 18s Scene 5 · Solution Demo — LaunchGrid Dashboard ▼
Spoken script
"LaunchGrid se — professional website ready in minutes. Online store with payment gateway built-in. WhatsApp Business connected. GST billing automatic. Sab ek jagah."
Visual — screen recording
Mobile screen recording of LaunchGrid dashboard. Rapid demo scroll — show: 1. Website preview (2s) 2. Products/store section (2s) 3. Payment toggle ON (1.5s) 4. WhatsApp icon connected (1.5s) 5. GST billing panel (1s)
Caption animations
Each feature gets a label that pops up as you swipe to it: — "✅ Website" — "✅ Online Store" — "✅ Payment Gateway" — "✅ WhatsApp" — "✅ GST Billing"
Edit — visual change rule
Screen changes every 1.5–2s by scrolling or tapping in the demo. No static shot held >2s during demo section.
Emotion
Aspiration + Convenience. Each checkmark is satisfying. Viewer imagines their own business with this.
Real dashboard proofFeature revealIndian business owner contextConvenience trigger
